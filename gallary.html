PhotoShop Edits

The Red Devil

The Red Devil was my first ever proper PhotoShop project. I was very happy with the finished result and it took a few hours to do. It is on bagged suspension, dropped to the floor. The deep dish wheels really set off the look of this car.

Wide Arch Wino

I wanted perfect the pop up lights on the futo. I added a front mount intercooler with some arch flares. I extended the front bumper showing off the futos good looks. It would make my day if we had this feature in Grand Theft Auto.

The Silver Bullet

Well what about that for fitment? I love these wheels in real life so I had to put them on a futo. It took a bit of time to get them to fit nicely but the result speaks for itself. I cleaned up back of the futo by debadging it .
