Introduction

Welcome to Futo Drift Masters. We are a Grand Theft Auto online drifting crew who are devoted to drifting the beautiful vehicle called the "Karin Futo". Established in Janury 2014, the crew consists of many chill and talented drifters. We are always sideways in our favourite car in the game.
